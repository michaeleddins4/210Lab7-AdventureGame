Adventure game!

By Michael Eddins

In this game, you play as a character, awakening inside of a maze with no recollection of why you are there. Your only objective is to escape!

The rooms in which health,points,strength, etc. change consist of:

  • (localhost:6003/room1) Changes health upon taking damage from the monster, strength and points go up on its defeat
  • (localhost:6003/room3) Changes health upon taking damage from the monster, strength and points go up on its defeat
  • (localhost:6003/room5) Changes health upon taking damage from the monster, strength and points go up on its defeat
  • (localhost:6003/room7) Changes points
  • (localhost:6003/room4) Changes health
  • (localhost:6003/room8) Changes strength
  • (localhost:6003/room6) Has a check for a certain number of strength to be able to progress
  • (localhost:6003/room9) Changes health upon taking damage from the boss, strength and points go up on its defeat
